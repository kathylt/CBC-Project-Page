BIKE LOCKER MANAGEMENT PROJECT

Learn More

A Informatics Capstone Project Sponsored by the Cascade Bicycle Club

For this project, we will be developing a relational database to store reserved bicycle locker data in King County as well as a database management front-end dashboard for CBC employees using noSQL and the Python web framework Django.

This project is a part of the Capstone Project course at the University of Washington Information School. For more information, visit this link here.

The Value of Bike Lockers

Benefits the Community

Bike lockers benefit the community and the city infrastructure. By providing designated places to stow bikes, sidewalks and business fronts are kept clear for the public.

Promotes Health and Wellness

Commuting via bike is a popular choice for many who love the the exercise and also love the relief of stress. By providing bicycle lockers, we are supporting commuters to ride more regularly by ensuring that they have a secure and safe location to store their bike.

Works Towards Environmental Sustainability

Bike lockers encourage bicycle commuting which has a great environmental impact. Working towards an increase in bicycle commuting is vital in reducing traffic congestion and air pollution.

  • Meet Our Sponsor

    Cascade Bicycle Club, the nation's largest statewide bicycle organization, is powered by 15,000 members and serves bike riders of all ages and abilities throughout the Puget Sound region and across Washington state.

Key Features in Our Solution

  • Robust Relational Database

    We are building a relational database with noSQL and Django. We have designed this specifically for CBC and have developed robust queries and stored procedures to execute all their business needs.

    Simple Database Management Dashboard

    We will create a management platform for CBC staff to handle data entry and updates. This dashboard will consolidate all of the database actions and provide the users with an non-technical interface to make changes to the database.

    The dashboard will also have the ability to run reports and generate simple data visualizations.

    Connection with Google Forms

    CBC currently uses Google Forms to handle bike locker renewals. We will create one for locker inquiries as well and connect these forms to our database to automatic this part of their business processes.
